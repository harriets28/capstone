About

Stories from the edges of the world.

Honest travel stories from people who've actually been there. No sponsored content, no pretense. Just stories that stick with you.

What is Wanderlust?

Wanderlust started as my personal travel journal, a place to write down the moments that stuck with me long after I'd come home. The unexpected conversations, the quiet mornings in unfamiliar cities, the ways a place can shift how you think about the world.

But I didn't want to keep this to myself. I wanted to create a space where other travelers could share their stories too; the real ones, not polished guidebook versions. Stories about the detours that led somewhere magical, the meals that surprised you, the encounters that changed how you see the world. A community of writers who've actually been there, who understand what it means to return home different.

How it works

Stories are published by our community of writers and contributors. Registered members can like stories, leave comments, and build a personal wishlist of destinations they'd love to visit. Profiles are public so you can see what other travellers are reading and dreaming about.
