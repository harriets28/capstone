About

Stories from the edges of the world.

Slow, honest travel writing. No sponsored content. No listicles. Just stories from the places we've been lucky enough to visit.

What is Wanderlust?

Wanderlust started as a personal travel journal and grew into a community of writers sharing stories from around the world. We believe the best travel writing doesn't just describe a place — it captures how a place makes you feel, what it teaches you, and why it stays with you long after you've left.

Every story on this site is written by someone who has actually been there — who has navigated the confusion of an unfamiliar city, eaten the food, got lost, found something unexpected, and come home changed.

How it works

Stories are published by our team of writers and contributors. Registered members can like stories, leave comments, and build a personal wishlist of destinations they'd love to visit. Profiles are public so you can see what other travellers are reading and dreaming about.
